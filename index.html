About The Clan

Paradox Gaming is a diverse group adult gamers from all walks of life. We are a mature and skilled clan, but our first priority has always been to have fun. We carry a simple mindset, Quality over quantity. We strive to give warm welcomes and to ensure there is always help available, no matter what game we may be playing. We aspire to be more than just a clan, we are a close knit community, a place where all are welcome, a place to find mature, helpful gamers much like ourselves.

Paradox Gaming is a friendly community, where we accept any guardians from the most experienced to the new players of the game. If you're looking for a community who is willing to help you through the game as well as a community which is friendly and isn't bothered about being inactive due to work or other social stuff then your looking in the right place.

We might be older than most, but don't let that fool you. We are not casual players.

THIS CLAN MIGHT BE FOR YOU IF:

  • You struggle to find people your age to game with and you're tired of dealing with children.
  • You want more mature conversation and friendships that extend beyond gaming.
  • You want a clan that supports each other in things like youtubing, streaming etc...
