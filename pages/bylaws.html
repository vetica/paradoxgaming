PARADOX CLAN BYLAWS


SECTION 1: GENERAL PURPOSE

  1. Paradox Gaming Clan (PDX) prides itself on being a mature and skilled clan, but our first priority has always been to have fun.
  2. Membership in PDX is open to all persons regardless of sex, race, religion or ethnic background.
  3. To ensure that all members of PDX have an equally enjoyable time in the clan, we have established a set of rules and guidelines that all members are obligated to follow. Let these serve as the official PDX bylaws.
    1. Any member that violates these bylaws will be subjected to a range of imposed sanctions including and up to termination of membership from the clan.
  4. These bylaws serve as the sole governing policies of PDX and are subject to review and interpretation by the Executive Board. These bylaws are subject to change at any time without notice or warning by the Executive Board.

SECTION 2: LEADERSHIP ROLES

  1. PDX leadership positions are: Chief Executive Officer, Executive Officer, Clan Officer.
    1. Chief Executive Officer (CEO) is primary leader of the PDX.
      1. The CEO holds a non-voting position on the Executive Board except in a tiebreaker situations.
      2. The CEO shall be elected and/or removed simple majority vote of the Executive Board and Clan Officers.
      3. CEO elections will be held annually.
    2. Executive Officers (EO) are the primary administrators of PDX.
      1. All EOs will serve on the Executive Board.
      2. EO elections will be held annually.
      3. The Executive Board shall vote on all clan business, membership approvals and disciplinary actions.
      4. EOs shall be elected by simple majority vote by all clan members.
      5. All EOs must have previously served as Officers.
    3. Clan Officers (CO) are the moderating members of the clan.
      1. COs are appointed by the Executive Board
      2. COs fulfill any tasks as assigned by the Executive Board and ensure that all executive directives are carried out.
  2. Administrators of games that offer clan support shall be either EOs or COs.
    1. If a clan member would like to start a PDX affiliate clan within a game that offers clan support, they should consult the CEO prior to its creation.

SECTION 3: MEMBERSHIP

  1. All members shall successfully pass the Recruitment Program prior to being granted full membership and in-game clan tag unless otherwise dictated by the Executive Board.
    1. The Recruitment Program
      1. New recruits will fill out a clan membership application. and be assigned a supervising CO who will oversee the Recruitment Program.
      2. Recruits will be given access to all clan communication platforms and must be active.
      3. Recruits will remain on a 30-day evaluation period. During the evaluation period, recruits should play with current clan members as often as possible.
      4. Recruits must play with the supervising and another CO and have a positive evaluation with a recommendation for full membership from both.
      5. After receiving two CO recommendations, recruits will remain on a 30-day probation period ending on a date assigned to them by their supervising CO. The probation can be waived at the discretion of the Executive Board.
      6. After the evaluation period is over, the Executive Board will decide if full clan membership is awarded.
        1. Recommendations from current clan members will weigh heavily on the recruits acceptance.
        2. If the Recruit is rejected, they can appeal the Executive Board decision to the CEO. The CEO will conduct a review and make the final ruling concerning clan membership. The CEO ruling is final.
  2. Former members seeking reentry to the clan will consult with the CEO. The CEO will evaluate the reentry applicant and create a personalized reentry program.

SECTION 4: CONDUCT

  1. All members shall treat other members with dignity and respect at all times.
    1. Members shall not exclude, belittle, slander, or otherwise negatively impact other members.
  2. Members shall remain professional and courteous at all times while in game and using the designated clan communication platforms.
  3. When representing the clan outside of the game or website, members shall remain just as professional and courteous.
  4. Members shall adhere to and abide by all rules, regulations, policies, procedures, practices, traditions, and rulings set forth by the administration and their designees.
  5. Members shall not form cliques or individual social groups within the clan that reflect negatively on the clan or members.
  6. Members shall not compromise the security or progression of the clan in any capacity.
  7. Members shall contribute to the clan to ensure progression, growth, and development.
  8. If a member is given a directive by an EO or CO they are required to follow the directive. Ignorance of the bylaws or designations is not an excuse.
  9. If a member develops negative relations with another member, the Executive Board must be notified and proper action will be taken.
    1. Members have the opportunity to report abuse within the clan to any administrative member and retain their anonymity during the administrative investigation.

SECTION 5: ACTIVITY

  1. All members shall remain active within clan activities and communication platforms.
    1. This requires all members to submit 15 substantial posts on the communication platforms every three months or complete 15 hours of in-game activity with the clan in three months.
      1. Any member that fails to meet this requirement during a three month time period will be issued a verbal warning. Subsequent offenses will result in termination of membership. (See Section 8)

SECTION 6: REPRESENTATION

  1. All members shall represent the official clan tag [PDX] or Paradox Gaming Clan name when representing the clan in game or on other duties where such representation is appropriate.
    1. Members shall not utilize the official clan tag where it is not authorized.
    2. Approval must be granted by the clan administration prior to representing the clan in any non-traditional capacity.

SECTION 7: OTHER CLAN AFFILIATION

  1. All members shall remain a loyal and active member of Paradox Gaming Clan and shall not join any other clan without approval from clan administration.
  2. It is acceptable for a member to join a clan on a different console or different game that PDX does not support at the time.
    1. However, if PDX adopts support for that game or that console in the future, the member is required to release membership from other clans and solely represent PDX.
    2. Members are required to acknowledge that PDX is their sole clan of choice and ensure that other affiliations do not impede their allegiance to PDX.
    3. If a clan member would like to start a PDX affiliate clan within a game that offers clan support, they should consult the CEO prior to its creation.
  3. Members that violate this bylaw will be asked to release membership or affiliation with other organizations or have their membership terminated.

SECTION 8: DISCIPLINARY ACTION

  1. If any member of PDX does something that is or can be considered unbecoming of a member or violate any rules, regulations or policies as set forth by these bylaws, the member will be subjected to administrative review and punishment.
    1. Possible punishments under these bylaws: Verbal warning, temporary ban, permanent ban and termination of membership, probationary status, demotion or any other suitable action deemed fit by the Executive Board.
    2. If a member receives disciplinary action, they can appeal the Executive Board decision to the CEO. The CEO will conduct a review and make the final ruling concerning the disciplinary action. The CEO can change, amend or reject the Executive Board decision. The CEO ruling is final.
